**Ai Studio (UI/UX Flash by Boo)** is a browser-based AI design tool that converts a single text prompt into three simultaneous, high-fidelity HTML/CSS UI variants — powered by Google Gemini. You can explore distinct visual directions side by side, lock in a favourite as your Base DNA to drive further iterations, and export production-ready code in seconds. No design software or coding experience required.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Navigate to Ai Studio in your browser — no installation needed. Your session history and saved artifacts are available right away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type a prompt">
    Describe the UI you have in mind. Include the screen type, style preferences, or target audience. The more context you give, the sharper the output.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a design">
    Review the three high-fidelity HTML/CSS variants generated side by side. Select your favourite as the **Base DNA** to anchor all future iterations, or choose one of 25+ design style variations to remix.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export or iterate">
    Download a ready-to-use ZIP of the HTML/CSS files, save the design to your artifacts library, or refine it further with a follow-up prompt.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All three variants are generated in a single request — you never have to re-run a prompt just to see alternative directions.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Set a Base DNA after your first generation to keep your brand's colours, typography, and layout consistent across every subsequent iteration.
</Tip>
