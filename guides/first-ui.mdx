By the end of this guide, you'll go from a completely blank prompt to a downloaded UI package — ready to drop into any project. Ai Studio (UI/UX FLASH BY BOO) uses Google Gemini to produce three high-fidelity HTML/CSS variants simultaneously, so your first generation gives you a real choice right out of the gate. Let's walk through the entire flow from boot to export.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch the app">
    Start your local dev server and open Ai Studio in your browser. You'll be greeted by an animated boot sequence as the system initialises. Wait a moment while **ARTIFACT_OS** loads — the starfield interface will fade in once everything is ready. You don't need to do anything here; the intro is automatic and sets the tone for the workspace you're about to use.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the prompt input">
    Look to the top of the screen and click **COMMAND ACCESS** in the header. The prompt input bar will slide up from the bottom of the viewport, ready for your instructions. This is your primary interface for communicating with the Gemini AI engine.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Type your prompt">
    Enter a clear, descriptive prompt for the UI you want to generate. For your first attempt, try something like:

    ```
    Design a sleek pricing table with three tiers: Free, Pro, and Enterprise
    ```

    The more detail you include — colours, tone, layout preferences — the closer the output will be to your vision. Not sure what to ask for? Press **Tab** to accept the animated placeholder suggestion currently cycling in the input field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your prompt">
    Press **Enter** or click the **EXECUTE** button to kick off generation. A burst of confetti will fire to confirm your submission, and the AI will begin working immediately. You'll see the interface transition into generation mode.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Watch generation unfold">
    Three artifact cards appear on screen simultaneously, each assigned a unique creative style name that streams in as it's coined. Inside each card, you can watch the HTML being written in real time — lines of code resolving into a fully rendered UI. All three variants generate in parallel, so you won't be waiting long.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Focus a result">
    Once generation completes, click the card that appeals to you most. This expands it to a full-screen interactive **iframe**, letting you scroll, hover, and interact with the design as if it were a live webpage. Take your time evaluating it — you can click other cards to switch focus at any point.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the source code">
    With an artifact focused, click **SOURCE** in the header to open a panel showing the raw HTML and CSS that powers the design. From here you can read through the code, click **Copy** to grab it to your clipboard, or click **Download** to save the file directly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export your artifact">
    When you're happy with what you've got, click **Download** in the header. Ai Studio will package and deliver a **ZIP file** containing:

    - The complete HTML file for your UI
    - A screenshot of the rendered design
    - A `README.md` with generation metadata
    - A `prompt.json` recording the exact prompt used

    Unzip the package and your UI is ready to integrate, share, or archive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Not sure what to build first? Click the **Execute Random Protocol** button (labelled "Surprise Me") to instantly submit whatever placeholder prompt is currently animating in the input bar. It's a great way to explore Ai Studio's range and get inspired before you start working on your own ideas.
</Tip>
