When you generate dozens of designs across multiple sessions, finding the right artifact later can quickly become a challenge. Ai Studio's **Library** gives you a permanent home for your best work, with named folders, colour-coded organisation, live previews, and a favorites system that surfaces the designs you return to most often. This guide covers everything you need to keep your workspace tidy and your best work within easy reach.

## Saving to the Library

The Library is the difference between an artifact that lives only in a session and one you can always find again. To save any design:

1. **Focus the artifact** you want to keep by clicking its card. It will expand to full-screen view.
2. Click **VAULT_PUSH** in the header. A panel will appear asking where to save it.
3. **Choose an existing folder** from the list, or select **Uncategorized** if you haven't created any folders yet.
4. Confirm the save. The artifact is now permanently stored in your Library and will survive page refreshes, browser restarts, and new sessions.

<Note>
  You can VAULT_PUSH any focused artifact at any stage — immediately after generation, mid-iteration, or after making revisions. Save early and often for designs you care about.
</Note>

## Creating folders

Folders let you group related designs by project, client, component type, or any system that suits your workflow. To create one:

1. Open the **Library** by clicking **NAV ACCESS** in the header to open the sidebar, then navigate to the Library view — or click **VAULT_PUSH** directly when an artifact is focused.
2. Click **NEW_DIR** and type a name for your folder.
3. The folder is created immediately and auto-assigned one of **8 colour labels** for quick visual distinction in the sidebar.

A simple folder structure goes a long way. Here's one approach to get you started:

| Folder | What to put in it |
|---|---|
| **Landing Pages** | Hero sections, full-page layouts, marketing screens |
| **Dashboards** | Admin panels, analytics views, data-heavy UIs |
| **Components** | Buttons, cards, modals, forms, nav bars |
| **Client Work** | Anything tied to a specific project or stakeholder |

You can create as many folders as you need and rename them at any time.

## Browsing the Library

The Library's left column lists **ALL** at the top followed by each of your named folders. Click **ALL** to see every saved artifact at once, or click a folder name to filter the view to just that folder's contents.

Each artifact tile in the main panel displays:

- A **live preview** of the rendered HTML design
- The **original prompt** that produced it
- The **style name** assigned during generation
- Any **folder** it belongs to

Scroll through tiles to visually browse your collection without opening anything — the live previews make it easy to recognise designs at a glance.

## Reopening saved work

Click any artifact tile in the Library to load it into a new working session. The design will open in full-screen focus exactly as you saved it. From there you can:

- Continue iterating by setting it as **Base DNA** and writing revision prompts
- View and copy its source code via **SOURCE**
- Export it again as a ZIP via **Download**
- Push it to a different folder with **VAULT_PUSH**

Nothing about the saved artifact is altered by reopening it — your Library copy stays intact.

## Using favorites

If certain designs stand out as references or benchmarks you'll come back to repeatedly, mark them with the **heart icon** visible on each artifact tile. Favorited artifacts:

- Are flagged with a heart in both the **Library** and **HISTORY** panel
- Can be filtered or spotted at a glance across both views
- Remain favorited across sessions — the status syncs between your history and your saved Library entries

Use favorites to mark your personal design bests, client-approved directions, or any artifact you want to be able to locate in seconds.

## Session history

Even artifacts you haven't explicitly saved to the Library are accessible through your session history. Open **NAV ACCESS** in the header and select the **HISTORY** panel to browse a chronological log of every generation session. Click any session entry to jump back to that moment and interact with the artifacts from that run.

History gives you a safety net — if you generated something interesting and forgot to VAULT_PUSH it, you can usually find it here and save it retroactively.

<Warning>
  Library data and session history are stored in your **browser's localStorage**. This means your saved designs are tied to the browser and device you're using — they won't automatically appear on another machine or after clearing your browser data. Always **export critical artifacts as ZIP files** using the Download button to ensure you have a permanent, portable backup that exists outside the browser.
</Warning>
